LIGHTS OUT is a puzzle game that can be played on a grid of lights.
The objective is to turn off all the lights on the grid.

To play the game, follow these steps:
1. Start with a grid of lights, some of which are turned on.
2. Clicking a light will toggle it, as well as the adjacent lights (up, down, left, and right).
3. The objective is to turn off all the lights on the grid.

Here are some tips to help you solve the puzzle:
1. Try to turn off the lights on the edge of the grid first.
2. If you get stuck, try to click random lights and see if it helps.
3. If you want a real challenge, try to solve the puzzle in as few moves as possible.

GOOD LUCK!
